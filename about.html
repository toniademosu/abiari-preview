About the Studio

Rooted in the belief that luxury should feel personal.

Founded by Dr. Kerry Adebowale

A Texas-based design firm transforming spaces into contemporary luxury environments since 2020.

Kerry leads every project with distinctive energy and dedication — integrating contemporary aesthetics with luxurious elements, decorative patterns, textured finishes, and curated accessories.

Her philosophy is simple: sophisticated design should be financially accessible. We work closely with each client to understand how they live, then design spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful — whether we’re styling a single room or remodeling an entire home.

The firm handles residential and commercial projects nationwide, creating inviting spaces that clients return to again and again.
